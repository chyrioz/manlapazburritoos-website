Q: Why did you make this restaurant?

I experimented a bit with the ingredients of Burritos, Tacos, and Shawarmas. I mixed the Burritos and Tacos' ingredients and it was very good.
I also mixed the ingredients of a Burrito and Shawarmas, and I liked this more. I gave a taste test to my family, and they all liked it very much.
Here I am, opening a restaurant regarding my experiments.

Q: I heard that there is a Cockatoo in the restaurant, shouldn't it be kept?

No, the Cockatoo's name is Argus and he is a well-mannered bird. He is extroverted and likes to be with alot of people.
I have thought that he should be able to interact to people because he becomes sad whenever he's lonely.
There are precautions with the bird by the way. It is just basic respect and do not try to pluck his wings or do anything bad to him.
